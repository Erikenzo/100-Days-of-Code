Simple PONG game.

Project was written using OOP and functions.

Main mission do not allow the ball to hit left or right wall.
If wall hits left wall player on the right gets the point and vice versa.
Use paddles to defend your side!
Left player controls paddle by following keys "w" - move up and "s" - move down.
Right player controls paddle by following keys "up-arrow" - move up and "down-arrow" - move down.
